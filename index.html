Welcome to Meesho

Shop trendy fashion at the best prices.

Meesho is an Indian social e-commerce marketplace founded in December 2015 by IIT Delhi graduates Vidit Aatrey and Sanjeev Barnwal. Headquartered in Bengaluru, it is designed to democratize internet commerce by connecting small businesses and individual entrepreneurs directly with customers across India.

Key Aspects of Meesho

Business Model: A multi-sided tech platform with a zero-commission model for sellers, relying on low-cost products and social media marketing.
Categories: Fashion, accessories, home & kitchen, beauty, electronics, personal care, and more.
Reseller Focus: Enables users to become resellers, earning profit by sharing products on social media.
Target Audience: Aims to serve diverse income backgrounds across India, especially in "Bharat" (rural India).
Technology: Leverages AI for personalized experiences and data processing (BharatMLStack).
Logistics: Integrates logistics partners (like its own Valmo) for efficient delivery.
Growth: Became one of India's largest marketplaces by orders, attracting significant users and investment.
